Sons para fazer tempo
Workshop at Jardins Efémeros, Viseu, 2016
Coordination - Inês Castanheira, Luis Eustáquio

In this workshop we cover several creative strategies to generate sounds from reused materials.
Starting with a brief contextualization, several exemplary projects are presented, and several possible approaches for using the available materials are demonstrated. Participants are strongly advised to bring all types of devices and materials that can be used and recycled to the workshop. From modified electronic devices to soundboxes, acoustic bags and refurbished objects, participants will explore the creation of sounds as a form of temporal manifestation. In the end, a musical performance is created in collaboration with the participants, based on the objects and instruments created in the meantime.
