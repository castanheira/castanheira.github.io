Inês Castanheira
Geradores


Workshop at Circuito Summer School, Gnration, Braga 2021
Coordination - Inês Castanheira

This workshop explores the DIY construction of electronic sound instruments based on the use of integrated circuits to generate sounds and noises. During one day, basic electronic techniques will be discussed, with each participant being able to build and customize their own sound instrument in a playful and experimental way. Note: note each participant must bring a 9V battery and a wooden or plastic box to serve as packaging for their instrument
