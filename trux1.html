Inês Castanheira
@c & Well-In Trux We Pux 01



In Trux We Pux is a compilation conducted by Favela Discos that aims to record and display a set of sound tendencies and collaborative practices, that have been developed in Porto's experimental and improvised music scene. This volume contains a selection of musicians who navigate through various fields of electronic music, that were invited to collaborate and produce a new track.

A1 - El Niño en el Utero Responde Arbusto de Bayas - Tito Silva, Filipe Silva
A2 - 129 / Steps @c - Miguel Carvalhais, Pedro Tudela Well - Inês Castanheira, João Sarnadas
A3 - Smoke Room ocp - João Ricardo Patches - André Miranda, Cláudio Oliveira, Daniel Assunção, João Soares, Marcelo Reis, Nelson Duarte, zero_one
B1 - XII COLETIVO VANDALISMO - Pedro Abrantes, Valdemar Pereira QUERIDO LIDER - Rui Fonseca
B2 - Demon Break Challenger - Luís Kasprzykowski Lorr No - Nuno Loureiro
