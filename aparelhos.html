Construção de Aparelhos Sonoros


Workshop at BMA Lab, Gnration, Braga 2022
Coordination - Inês Castanheira

In this BMA Lab, some of the main concepts and basic electronic techniques used in the DIY construction of sound electronic instruments will be presented. Throughout the workshop, each participant will have contact with different types of electronic tools and components through the assembly and creative experimentation of electronic circuits, building and customizing their own sound instrument, which they will take with them at the end.
